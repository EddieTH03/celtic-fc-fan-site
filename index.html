About Celtic FC

Celtic Football Club is a professional football club located in Glasgow, Scotland. The club was founded in 1887 by Brother Wolfrid to alleviate the poverty in the Irish-Scots population in the East End of Glasgow. During the 1960s and 1970s, the club enjoyed a lot of success under Jock Steinâ€™s management, including becoming the first British club to win the UEFA European Cup in 1967, beating FC Internazionale Milano 2-1 in Lisbon, thus immortalising this team as The Lisbon Lions. As of the start of the 2024-2025 season, Celtic have won 54 Scottish League titles, 42 Scottish FA Cups and 21 Scottish League Cups. The team is managed by Brendan Rodgers, and captained by Callum McGregor.
